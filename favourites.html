Favourite Downloads and Links

Sun 26 June 2016

Ramakrishna Vedanta Literature

  • RamakrishnaVivekananda.info includes Complete works of Swami Vivekananda, various Kathamrita/Gospel versions and a few biographies.
  • Sri Sri Ramakrishna Kathamrita - English translation by Dharm Pal Gupta
  • Complete works of Swami Vivekananda on Belur Math site - Belur Math site
  • Raja Yoga by Swami Vivekananda includes a running translation of Yoga Sutra of Patanjali
  • Collection of various books on Ramakrishna and Vedanta Literature - Brazil Collection. Includes various Upanishads, Brahma sutras, Gita, Great Master and so much more.
  • Ramakrishna Vedanta Literature - Google Drive Collection. Mostly in Bangla script.
  • Sri Sri Ramakrishna Kathamrita Centenary Memorial
  • Sri Ramakrishna: the Great Master - by Swami Saradananda. Translated by Swami Jagadananda.

Biographies

  • वेदमूर्तिः श्रीरामकृष्णः
  • Collection of Biographies of Indian Saints and Heroes in Sanskrit
  • Shankara Divijaya by Vidyaranya
  • Shankara Digvijaya with Sanskrit Tika
  • The Traditional Life of Shankaracharya - English translation of Shankara Digvijaya

Sanskrit Grammar

  • संस्कृत व्याकरण पुस्तक संग्रह - archive.org collection. Includes Ashtadhyayi, Dhatupatha, Kashika, Mahabhashya, Prathamavritti, Ramnath Sharma Ashtadhyayi, Laghu Kaumudi and others
  • Sanskrit grammar books - OneDrive Collection

Concordances and Dictionaries

  • Upanishad Vakya Kosha - A Concordance of the Principal Upanishads and Bhagavad Gita
  • Critical Word Index to Bhagavadgita by Katre

Gita

  • गीता (मूलमात्रम्)
  • Gita - Gita Press Gorakhpur on archive.org
  • Gita - Swami Swarupananda
  • Gita Supersite at IITK - Gita direct link
  • Online reference to Gita
  • Gita Supersite has links to Gita, Brahma Sutras, Valmiki Ramayana, Yoga Sutra, Narada Bhakti Sutra, Minor Gitas.

Chandi

  • Durga Saptashati - Swami Jagadishwarananda

  • दुर्गासप्तशती with seven commentaries. Part 1, Part 2, Part 3

Mahabharata

Gita Press (translation into Hindi by Ramanarayanadatta astri)

  • Volume 1: Adi Parva (1) and Sabha Parva (2)
  • Volume 2: Vana Parva (3) and Virata Parva (4)
  • Volume 3: Udyoga Parva (5) and Bhishma Parva (6)
  • Volume 4: Drona (7), Karna (8), Shalya (9), Sauptika (10) and Stri Parvas (11)
  • Volume 5: Shanti Parva (12)
  • Volume 6: Anushasana (13), Ashvamedika (14), Ashramavasika (15), Mausala (16), Mahaprasthanika (17) and Svargarohana (18) Parvas
