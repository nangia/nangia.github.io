Conducting Technical Interviews

Sat 04 May 2013

My Experience with Interviews

As part of my job, I have been conducting technical interviews for almost 17 or 18 years now as part of the different companies I have worked for. And of course, I have been interviewed during the course of these years many times - either when I was looking for a new job or as when clients wanted to talk to me because we wanted to bag a new outsourcing project. Primarily there are the following kinds of interviews:

  • Tresspassers will be Recruited: This happens when the organization requires so many people that all the hiring managers and HR are under tremendous pressure to take in people.
  • Testing in Phases
  • Testing all together
  • Our organization is the most Superior
  • Too many candidates at Hyderabad

Lessons Learnt

  • Be not Afraid. Big Consultant. IIT graduate
  • Do you want to hire a senior guy?
  • Time up
